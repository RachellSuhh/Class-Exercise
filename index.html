Diptyque's Best Scented Candles

Baies

The Baies (Berries) scented candle speaks of harmony - a coming together of flower and fruit.

Notes

The tangy coolness of a few fresh-picked blackcurrant berries. Lively, flowery rose accents.

Characteristics

Suitable for small and medium-sized rooms

Fragrance gradually released and persistent (optimal after around 20 minutes).

There is no limit on the size of the space as long as you keep an eye on the candle.

Burning time: around 50 hours
